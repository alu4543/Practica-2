Conversion Formula

To convert from ºC to ºF : ºF = ºC x 1.8 + 32.

To convert from ºF to ºC : ºC = (ºF-32) ÷ 1.8.

To convert from K to ºC : ºC = K – 273.15.

To convert from ºC to K : K = ºC + 273.15.

To convert from ºF to K : K = 5/9 (ºF – 32) + 273.15.

To convert from K to ºF : ºF = 1.8(K – 273.15) + 32.
